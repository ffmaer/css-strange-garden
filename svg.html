Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG)

        

        SVG defines 6 types of path commands, for a total of 20 commands:

        MoveTo: M, m
        LineTo: L, l, H, h, V, v
        Cubic Bézier Curve: C, c, S, s
        Quadratic Bézier Curve: Q, q, T, t
        Elliptical Arc Curve: A, a
        ClosePath: Z, z
