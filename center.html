Using HTML+CSS to center a DIV has haunted programmers since 1996.
The margin-left+translateX method seems over-complicated for such a common and conceptually simple task.
First of all, use a flex-box container to put a div (a column) at the center.
Secondly, apply align-items: center to the flex-box, overriding the default (align-items: stretch).
Thirdly, use text-align: center to center the texts.
